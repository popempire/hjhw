Spirits
-------

- **Vodka**
  neutral spirit (typically unaged)
  - Smirnoff No. 21
- **Gin**
  neutral spirit + redistillation (typically unaged)
  - Gordon's London Dry
- **Rum**
  sugarcane spirit (sometimes aged)
  - Don Q Cristal
  - Bacardi Anejo Cuatro
- **Mezcal** (Tequila)
  agave spirit (sometimes aged)
  - El Jimador Tequila
- **Whiskey**
  grain (malted barley, corn, rye, etc.) spirit (typically aged)
  - Evan Williams Kentucky Straight Bourbon (black label)
  - Old Granddad Kentucky Straight Bourbon
  - Wild Turkey 101 Kentucky Straight Bourbon
- **Brandy**
  fruit (grapes/wine unless otherwise noted) spirit (typically aged)
  - St. Remy VSOP
