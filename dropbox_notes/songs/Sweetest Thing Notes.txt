Sweetest Thing Notes
====================

cf. negative theology, since we cannot describe or comprehend God, we can only speak about what God is not

Sartre the being-for-itself is in the manner of not-being what it is

at the risk of conflating distant ideas on the basis of superficial similarities:

Thinkers of many times and places have understood that our biggest and most important dreams concern the structures and matrices that support and give form to our concepts. Inasmuch as these visions challenge the structures by which we interpret meaning and craft language, they must remain somehow ineffable in that mode of thought or language.
