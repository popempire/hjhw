{{ page.title }}
================

 - __1 lb__ is 450 g (sometimes 500 g)
 - __1 pt__ is 480 ml (sometimes 500 ml)
 - __1 oz__ is 28 g or 30 ml
 - __1 T__  is 15 ml
 - __1 t__ or 
   __1 tsp__ is 5 ml
 - __1 C__  is 240 ml (sometimes 250 ml)
 - __1 qt__ is 0.95 l (sometimes 960 ml)
 - __1 gal__ is 3.8 l
 - __1 in__ is 25 mm
 - __1 ft__ is 0.3 m
 - __1 mi__ is 1.6 km
 - __1 kg__ is 2.2 lb
 - __1 km__ is 5/8 mi (5 furlongs)
